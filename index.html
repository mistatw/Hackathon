EVENT

Why this hackathon?

Push your limits. Test your knowledege

Hackathons are proving grounds for new ideas. They’re especially good tools to stimulate the creative and problem-solving juices of developers. Unlike their day jobs where risk-taking may be frowned upon, in a hackathon there is a low cost of failure.


 Prizes 


⬢ Top 3 teams will be awarded with cash prize upto ₹ 1 lakh.

⬢ Top 10 best teams will be nominated and special prizes are provided.

⬢ E- certificate will be provided to all participants.

Rules


⬢ College students from India are eligible to participate.
 
⬢ Pre-registration is mandatory for participation.

⬢ Maximum of 3 members per team irrespective of their academic streams. 

⬢ Mandatory to install Zoom app in advance to the event date.

⬢ Hardware / Software projects can be presented .
